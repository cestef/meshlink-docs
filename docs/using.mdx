# Using MeshLink

## Sharing your user ID

To share your user ID, tap the `Share` button in the top right corner of the app. This will open a share menu, where you can select an app to share your user ID with. You can also copy your user ID to the clipboard by simply tapping on it.

## Scanning for nearby devices

The scan is automatically started when you open the app. You can also manually stop and start the scan
by pressing the Button bottom right.

## Connecting to a device

You don't need to do anything to connect to a device. When you scan for nearby devices, MeshLink will automatically connect to them. You can then chat with them by clicking on their name.

## Manually add a device

You can manually add someone via their user ID by click on the `+` button in the bottom left corner. You can then paste their user ID into the text field and click `Add`.

## Sending a message

To send a message, click on the device you want to send the message to. You can then type your message, and click on the send button.

## Blocking an user

To block an user, click on their profile picture in the chat. You can then click on the `Block` button to block them.

**⚠️ Please note that you will still retransmit messages from them to other users, but you won't receive any direct messages from them.**

## Deleting a conversation

Long press on the device on the scan list you want to delete the conversation with. You can then click on the `Delete` button to delete the conversation.

## Deleting all data from the app

To delete all data, you can simply uninstall the app. This will delete all data from the app (including your user ID).

Alternatively, you can also go to the settings and click on the `Delete all data` button.

## Changing your user ID

You cannot change your user ID. If you want to change it, you will have to uninstall the app and reinstall it.
